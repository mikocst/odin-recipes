Homemade Smash Burger Recipe

Smash Burger Recipe

This smash burger recipe makes super juicy burgers with crispy edges. Don't use meat that is leaner than 80/20 for best results, and make sure the meat is very cold. I prefer to cook these outdoors to avoid smoking up the kitchen. These grill up very fast because of the high heat, so make sure you have everything ready to go!

Ingredients

  • 4 Hamburger Buns
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter, softened, or as needed
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef (80% lean)
  • 4 6-inch squares of parchment paper
  • salt to taste
  • 4 slices of American Cheese
  • Burger toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat outdoor grill and lightly oil the grate. Set a cast iron onto the grill to preheat until smoking.
  2. Step 2: Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast the flat-top until lightly browned then set aside.
  3. Step 3: Form meat into 8 loosely packed balls which weigh up to 2 ounces each. Place each ball on top of the pan and cover with parchment paper. Smash down the meat and sprinkle the meat with salt.
  4. Step 4: Grill for 45 seconds until the edges are dark brown and the centers are a light pink color. Grill for another 15-20 seconds and stack the patties over cheese. Move each stack to a bun with your favorite toppings.
